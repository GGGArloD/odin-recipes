Vinaigrette

French vinaigrette with a twist

Vinaigrette is a French word, so it must be done the French way

Ingredients

  • Olive oil - 4 soup spoons
  • Fresh squeeze lemon & lime juice - 2 soup spoons
  • Salt & pepper - 5 mill turns each
  • Smoked paprika - 1/2 tbl spoon
  • Dijon mustard - 1 tbl spoon
  • Shallots - 2 medium
  • Fresh basil and mint leaves - approx. 10 leaves each

Steps

  1. Find a ceramic or glass cereal bowl
  2. Pour the oil, citrus juice and spices into the bowl
  3. Beat all the ingredients together with a fork until the oil and citrus mix well
  4. Add the Dijon mustard and repeat the process until the mixture is smooth
  5. Chop the shallots into thin stripes (not cubes)
  6. Tap the leaves in your hands, like you applaud someone and cut them in thin stripes
  7. Incorporate the shallots and the leaves to the mix and stir gently
  8. Pour the vinaigrette on a roughly chopped lettuce or other fresh crisp veggies of your choice
  9. Sit down, relax and enjoy!

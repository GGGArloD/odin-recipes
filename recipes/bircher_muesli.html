Bircher Muesli

The breakie that keeps you full of energy all day long!

Are you already hungry an hour after you've finished your breakfast? Are you low on energy during the first part of your day? This time frame is critical and is when your best work is getting done! Maximize your productivity with this delicious bowl of goodies!

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats - 125g
  • Dried fruits (dates, apricots, figs, red berries) - 1 each, 3-5 berries
  • Brazilian and Macadamia nuts - 3 each, raw, not salted
  • Greek yoghurt - 125g
  • Soy milk - 250ml
  • Acacia honey - 1 tbl spoon
  • Flaxseed - 1 tbl spoon
  • Ground cinnamon - a pinch

Steps

  1. Dice the dried fruits into small pieces, roughly the size of a chickpea
  2. Crush the nuts into small pieces by pressing them with a big knife against a chopping board
  3. Add everything into a cereal bowl, along with the oats, the flaxseed and the cinnamon powder
  4. Pour the soy milk on top and stir gently
  5. Cover the bowl with cling film and set it to rest overnight in the fridge
  6. Before serving, gently mix a spoon of acacia honey into greek yoghurt
  7. Pour the yoghurt over the muesli and give the whole mix yet another gentle stir
  8. Enjoy cold or let it sit at room temperature for a bit!
